Mission

To educate students about Computer Science through seminars and lessons and allow them to apply their knowledge in events and competitions.

Activities

Computer Science Club prepares students for 3 different types of competitions: CTFs, CyberPatriot, and algorithmic programming competitions - as well as having a CS 101 workshop on Tuesdays that focuses on teaching various CS topics and building up attendees knowledge about foundational computer science topics.

Workshop Schedule

- CS 101 workshops are held on Tuesdays after school in Dr. Shukla's room (5.033) from 3:15 to 4 pm

- Cyber dragons workshops are held on Thursdays after school in Mr. Schepens's room (5.011) from 3:15 to 4 pm

- Algorithmic programming workshops are held on Fridays after school in Ms. Rachkovskiy's room (5.037) from 3:15 to 4 pm
